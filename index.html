I'm a technological problem solver who specializes as a creator and software engineer.

I discovered my passion for teaching and building open-source technology during my Computer Science undergraduate degree at UofT's Mississauga campus. Currently, I am the founder and president of Grey Software, a not-for-profit organization that empowers students to build open-source software for their communities and societies. https://grey.software
